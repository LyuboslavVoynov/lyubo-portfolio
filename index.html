What I Do

The work that actually moves the needle.

Most ops problems aren't technology problems. They're process problems dressed up as technology problems. I fix both.

AI-Enabled Workflow Automation

If your team is spending hours a week on tasks that follow a pattern — routing tickets, chasing data, sending updates, generating reports — those tasks shouldn't involve a human. I map the work, find what's automatable, and build systems that handle it reliably, 24/7. The result: your team spends their time on the things that actually need them.

Teams typically recover 6–12 hours per person per week within the first 90 days.
Core Offering

Customer Operations Redesign

Customer support that's slow, inconsistent, or expensive to run is a problem that compounds over time. I redesign the full CX workflow — from how tickets are triaged, to how agents are supported, to how escalations are handled — using AI to cut the work without cutting the quality. Customers get faster answers. Your team gets breathing room.

Past projects have delivered 50–87% reductions in resolution time and CSAT jumps of 20+ points.
High Impact

Operational Systems & Visibility

Scaling fast is hard when nobody can see what's actually happening across the business. I build the operational infrastructure that makes growth manageable — clear processes, structured data, dashboards that tell you what matters, and systems that hold up when volume doubles. No more firefighting. No more decisions made on gut feel alone.

Companies typically cut operational overhead by 30–40% while improving decision speed across teams.
